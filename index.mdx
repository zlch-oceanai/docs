## What you can build

- **Music generation** — Create original tracks from text prompts or style tags
- **Cover & remix** — Transform existing clips with new style and lyrics
- **Audio processing** — Separate stems, export MIDI, get lyrics timeline
- **AI Singer** — Create a custom AI singer persona from any clip
- **Sound effects** — Generate custom loops and one-shot sound effects
